mScroll

“mScroll” is an application designed and developed that will allow the title insurance company to disperse tasks/orders to qualified local abstractors and track progress. Once the abstractor has completed the task/orders and passed the quality checks, the application automatically triggers the payment processing. mScroll app also solicit feedback from the parties involved. Thus, the app enhances the Abstractor Transaction process to reduce the turnaround time and improves efficiency through automation & technology.

Features -

  • mScroll Client Dashboard :

    A dashboard for clients to perform administrative and backend tasks like adding and managing vendors, activate/deactivate accounts, add users with different permissions to manage the client account, manage payment processing for the orders complete, ,view BI analysis reports and dashboards pulled on orders and vendors which enable the client to take strategic decisions to efficiently manage the order distribution ,vendor order assignment etc…which helps in business decisions.

  • mScroll Vendor Dashboard:

    A dashboard for vendors to perform administrative and backend tasks like adding and managing service providers or abstractors, activate/deactivate accounts, assign orders to service providers/abstractors, track and manage orders throughout the order life cycle, manage payment processing for the orders complete, view BI analysis reports and dashboards pulled on orders and vendors which enable the vendor to take strategic decisions to efficiently manage the order distribution ,abstractor order assignment etc…which helps in business decisions.

  • mScroll Mobile app:

    mScroll mobile application is used by the service providers/abstractors to receive and communicate the status of orders. The use of mobile application brings transparency to the lifecycle of the order for the vendor and client. The purpose of the mobile application is to increase efficiency of order assignment and order status reporting by eliminating field abstractor dependency on workstations to receive new orders and to communicate.
